
# Hamsterdam — Master Production Blueprint

## Overview
* Concept: An 80s-sitcom-style parody featuring photorealistic, live-action hamsters living in a whimsical, 2D watercolor-animated Amsterdam.
* Vibe: Nostalgic, heartwarming, and quirky. The show centers on "poor little orphan" hamsters who navigate life, trades, and mishaps in a miniature, stylized version of the city.
* Audio Identity: Episodes utilize a whimsical, PBS-style kids' show glockenspiel instrumental background, layered with ambient sounds, hamster chatter, and squeaks. Singing/lyrics are omitted to allow for the use of your existing series intro video.

## Core Characters
* Barnaby: The older, larger orange-and-white hamster.
* Little Pip: The partner hamster.
* Permanent Uniform: Both hamsters consistently wear denim overalls and straw hats for all daily tasks and adventures.

## Production Strategy: Standalone Episode Format
Each episode is a standalone 12-second clip designed to be appended to your existing series intro video.
* Structure: Each video focuses on a single narrative beat.
* Ending: Episodes conclude with natural character interactions (e.g., nodding or moving toward a goal), explicitly avoiding 80s freeze-frames.

## Master "Constraint Sandwich" Template
Use this structure when prompting for new content:
1. Subject: Clearly identify Barnaby (larger, orange/white) and Little Pip wearing their permanent denim overalls and straw hats.
2. Action/Setting: Describe the watercolor-animated Amsterdam environment and the specific task or movement.
3. Technical/Audio: Specify "HD, 4k, cinematic" quality. Explicitly state the audio as a "whimsical, PBS-style kids' show glockenspiel instrumental," including ambient hamster chatter/squeaks, with no singing or lyrics.
