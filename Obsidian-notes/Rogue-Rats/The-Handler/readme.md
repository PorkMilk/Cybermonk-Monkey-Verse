
# The Handler

**Role:** Second-in-Command
**Faction:** [[Rogue Rats - General]]

## Function
Oversees field cells; ensures deniability and enforces security. Manages field operations and maintains the subterranean communication grid.

## Appearance
- Broad-shouldered, physically imposing
- Matted, oil-stained fur
- Jagged scars, one shredded ear
- Jaundice-colored eyes

## Aesthetic — WWI Trench Raider
- Distressed canvas tunic (repurposed workwear), reinforced with black rubber strips
- DIY gas mask made from a medical bulb and medicine bottle caps
- Bandoliers of battery cells
- Utility belt of leather and copper wire
- Fingerless gauntlets made from bicycle tires

## Tactical Focus
Field management and maintenance of the subterranean communication grid ([[Rogue Rats - General|The Network]]).
