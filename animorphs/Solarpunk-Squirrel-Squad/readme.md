

# Solarpunk Squirrel Squad — General

## Overview
**Title:** Captain Caliber
**Concept:** Solarpunk universe set in a suburban Central Texas neighborhood, featuring an Eastern Gray Squirrel and his highly disciplined squad.
**Philosophy:** "The Watchman Doctrine" — defensive neighborhood vigilance. The backyard is a strategic stronghold requiring constant audit and protection.
**Aesthetics:** Serious, professional military realism.
**Catchphrase:** "In the Name of David!"
**Tech Level:** Solarpunk integration — repurposed high-tech materials, bio-polymers, flexible solar cells.

## Squad Hierarchy
Inspired by Texas State Guard ethos — operates as a volunteer "Defense Force" for the neighborhood, focused on local community emergency support and sector security.

| Rank | Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|---|
| Commander | [[Captain Caliber (character)]] | Strategic oversight; reports to "The Governor" (Neighborhood Human Authority) |
| Operations Officer | Tactical Lead | Manages regional security; executes field missions |
| Logistics Specialist | Engineer | Resource distribution; maintains gear and "garrison" |
| Comms/Intelligence | Signals Officer | Monitors sector traffic; maintains silent communication |

## Narrative Structure — "Backyard Ecosystem" Model
Hub-and-spoke model for short-form media:
- **The Core:** Captain Caliber and his squad (the protagonists)
- **The Periphery:** Neighborhood creatures (e.g., Avian Lookout Network, Feline Neighborhood Watch) acting as assets, informants, or threats
- **Tactical Focus:** Day-to-day patrols, bird feeder audits, perimeter security

## Outreach Strategy
- "Fun Fact" overlays in videos highlighting real-world volunteer principles (e.g. communication relays in emergencies), while maintaining a strict firewall between fiction and official military branding
- **Legal compliance:** Work of fiction, not affiliated with/endorsed by the Texas Military Department

## Status
Active, prioritized under the Watchman Doctrine.
